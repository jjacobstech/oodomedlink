# OODOMEDLINK

## Description

OODOMEDLINK is a web application that allows diagnostics clinics manage patient results and give patients the access to their results without having to go to the hospital and wait for the results.

✅ About Us
Oodo Medlink is a digital platform that helps medical laboratories send test results to patients instantly. The idea was inspired by real challenges many Nigerians face—long wait times, repeated visits to collect printed results, and outdated systems that slow down treatment.
We make result delivery fast, secure, and stress-free. With Oodo Medlink, labs upload results in seconds, patients receive them instantly, and healthcare becomes more efficient for everyone.

✅ Features
Instant Delivery – Upload and send results directly to patients via WhatsApp or email.
Secure Storage – Safely store digital copies of all results in an organized, encrypted system.
AI Result Explanation – Provide simple summaries to help patients understand their results.
Automated Notifications – Notify patients immediately when results are ready.
Performance Reports – Monthly insights to help labs track efficiency.
Multi-Branch Support – Manage multiple lab locations from one dashboard.
Staff Training – We guide lab teams to use the platform easily.
